About Me

Hi, my name is Gabriel Galvez and I make websites! I consider myself both a web designer and front end developer. In a nutshell, this means that I work with clients on design aspects like layout, fonts, and colors, and I also do the coding necessary to bring the website to life. I consider myself a “one stop shop”, in that I work with clients to understand their needs, design their website, fill it in with their content, code the site, set up web hosting, and anything else needed to get their website online, working properly, and looking great!

If I’m home and not working on a website, I’m probably either feeding a hungry cat or watching retro commercials (from the 80’s and 90’s) among other weird videos on Youtube. I also enjoy running at various parks and open spaces in Contra Costa County. I can also be found at the tennis court fairly often.
